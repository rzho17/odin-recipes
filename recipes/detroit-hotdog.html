Detroit-Style Coney Dogs

All about the dog!

No game is complete without a Detroit Coney. It's a grilled, natural-casing hot dog, loaded with chili sauce, mustard, and onions. Yummy...Detroit-style yummy!

Ingredients

  • hot dogs with natural casings
  • 1 hot dog rolls, sliced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 (10 ounce) can chile sauce without beans
  • 4 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard, or to taste

Directions

  1. Step 1: Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.
  2. Step 2: Place hot dogs on the grill; cook until browned, 5 to 8 minutes, turning once, or until done to suit your taste. Lightly grill hot dog rolls.
  3. Step 3: Meanwhile, place the chile sauce in a small microwave-safe bowl; cook 1 minute. Stir, and cook 1 minute more.
  4. Step 4: Place hot dogs on buns. Top each with chile sauce, onion, and 1 tablespoon mustard, or to taste.
